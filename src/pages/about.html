Our Story

About CLICK CLUTCH

BSIT student and CODM player passionate about tech, strategy, and growth.

EST. 2026

Background

Born From Passion

CLICK CLUTCH Esports was founded in 2026 by college friends who believed Southeast Asia had world-class CODM talent waiting to be discovered. Starting with a single squad and a second-hand gaming setup, we competed in local tournaments and scraped every resource to grow.

Two years later, three national titles, major sponsorships, and six active divisions. Today, CLICK CLUTCH is a fully professional organization with dedicated coaches, analysts, and a training facility — but we never forget our roots.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

🎯

Our Mission

To develop world-class CODM athletes from Southeast Asia by providing elite training, professional infrastructure, and a culture of excellence that turns raw talent into championship performance.

🔭

Our Vision

To become Asia's most respected esports brand — a team global audiences tune in to watch, and aspiring players dream of joining.

💡

Our Approach

Science-backed training, psychological coaching, and constant film review. We treat esports as a professional sport — because it is.
